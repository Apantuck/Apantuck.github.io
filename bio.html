The Life of Alex Pantuck:

I was originally born in New Jersey (but please don't tell anyone about it). When I was 1, my family moved to LA. In truth, I was a slow child, but, luckily, my elementary school principle, Joel Pelsiger, was confident that each of us needed to learn at our own pace. Thanks to him, I was able to foster a passionate curiosity and interest in science. In middle school (the dark ages), I had my first experience coding at ID-Tech summer camp (we made text adventures in C++). Later, in highschool (the slightly less dark ages), I took a programming class in Java. Yuck! But still, it helped foster my interest in computer science. Thanks Paul Way! Then there was an interlude involving an existential crisis (several, actually), but I made it out on the other side confident that I wanted to study computer science. So here we are. The end?
